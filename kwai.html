User Research Product Design 2025

Kwai App

A comprehensive user research and product design study focused on improving the short-video experience for Kwai's growing international user base.

Overview

Kwai is one of the world's largest short-video platforms with hundreds of millions of active users. This project focused on understanding pain points in the content discovery and creation flows, and redesigning key surfaces to improve engagement and creator retention.

Research Methods

We conducted in-depth interviews with 24 users across three markets, ran diary studies over two weeks, and synthesized insights using affinity mapping. Key themes emerged around discoverability, algorithmic trust, and the gap between passive viewers and active creators.

Usability testing sessions were run across both mobile platforms with participants from diverse backgrounds to ensure inclusive design decisions.

Design Outcomes

Based on our research, we redesigned the creator onboarding flow, introduced a contextual recommendation system, and simplified the editing tools to lower the barrier for new creators. Prototypes were validated through two rounds of usability testing before handoff to engineering.
