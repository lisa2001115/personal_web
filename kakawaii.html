Kaka&Waii

A brand identity and user experience design project for a playful lifestyle brand at the intersection of kawaii culture and everyday expression.

Brand Concept

Kaka&Waii emerged from a desire to bring joy and playfulness into everyday objects. The brand draws from Japanese kawaii aesthetics while grounding itself in accessible, cross-cultural visual language that resonates with Gen Z consumers.

Research & Strategy

User interviews and cultural analysis informed a brand strategy centered on emotional warmth, self-expression, and community. We mapped competitive brand landscapes and identified a clear positioning gap for a brand that was simultaneously cute, sophisticated, and globally accessible.

Identity System

The resulting identity system includes a custom typeface pairing, a pastel-forward color palette with bold accent moments, and an illustration system featuring original characters that appear across packaging, digital touchpoints, and social content.
