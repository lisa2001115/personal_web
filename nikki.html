Infinity Nikki

Visual and graphic design contributions to Infinity Nikki, an open-world fashion game — spanning UI illustration, in-game assets, and marketing materials.

Project Context

Infinity Nikki is a large-scale open-world game developed by Infold Games. As part of the visual design team, I contributed to building a cohesive aesthetic language across in-game UI, character visual concepts, and promotional graphic materials.

Visual Design

The visual system had to balance fantasy with fashion-forward sensibility. I developed a series of illustrated assets for in-game events, seasonal campaigns, and collectible card sets. The illustration style was refined over multiple iteration cycles in collaboration with the art director.

Special attention was paid to color harmony across different in-game environments and lighting conditions, ensuring assets felt native to the game world while maintaining brand consistency.

Graphic Design

On the graphic design side, I created promotional posters, social media templates, and event landing page layouts. These materials needed to feel lush and editorial while communicating key game mechanics and story beats clearly to new audiences.
